About us

Ms waffles is an established small business that offers a wider range of confectioneries. Mallory and Segan ratified their business in Durban, South Africa and extended their franchise to Johannesburg. The business was founded in 2019 and launched on the 7th of April. In trying to bring something new Ms waffles branded itself by adding a modern twist to traditional Belgium waffle. Thus, the main attraction are gourmet waffles served on a stick. Ms waffles makes use of Belgium chocolate to define the taste of their waffles. Together with its array of sensational toppings, Ms waffles is able to cater to everyone who enters through its doors. Hens the success of this business is not only marked by the taste of their product but also by the passion of its makers.

The secret ingredient

The recipe for the delicious waffle batter was perfected in the kitchen of a dedicated mother and father who supported their daughter's business endeavors. The result was the most exquisite "crispy on the outside, fluffy on the inside" waffle you have ever tasted, thanks to the mother's generous helping of imagination, indulgence, flavor, and love.
